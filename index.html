The Story

A Gunman's Redemption

After years of violence, Sawyer Bain hung up his guns and settled down with his wife, Eleanor, on a small farm outside of Half-a-Rock Hill. For three years, he found peace.

But when Eleanor vanishes without a trace, Sawyer must confront the darkness he thought he'd left behind. The townspeople whisper of an ancient evil that stirs after sundown, and the local sheriff refuses to help.

Armed with his old revolvers and a desperate heart, Sawyer ventures into Half-a-Rock Hill as night falls—unaware that some things are better left undisturbed, and that seven prayers might not be enough to save his soul.

"The town of Half-a-Rock Hill holds secrets darker than the night itself."

Meet the Creator

Tom Tuttle

Tom Tuttle is an award-winning comic creator known for his unique blend of western and horror genres. With over a decade of experience in the industry, his previous works include the critically acclaimed "Dust Devils" series and "The Hangman's Daughter."

"Seven Amens Before Sundown is my most personal project to date. It explores themes of redemption, sacrifice, and the monsters we all carry inside us. I've been developing this story for years, and I'm excited to finally bring it to life with your support."
